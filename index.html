Akshay Daydar

Brief Description: Akshay Daydar is a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati). His research interests include biomedical image processing, deep learning, and human gait analysis. Prior to joining IIT Guwahati, Akshay completed his postgraduate and undergraduate studies at the National Institute of Technology Warangal (NITW) and Government College of Engineering, respectively. He has also interned at BHEL Corporate R&D Hyderabad for his postgraduation project. Besides his academic pursuits, he enjoys photography, sketching, and traveling.
